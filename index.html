SPTA Dues Collection at SFES Basketball Court

Date: September 5, 2025 · Venue: SFES Basketball Court

The SPTA held an on-site dues collection day at the SFES basketball court to make it easier for parents to contribute. The activity was spearheaded by Treasurer Leizelyn Agoy-Agoy with support from SPTA officers and school staff. Official receipts were issued and entries were encoded for posting on this portal.

As of the latest report, total collections stand at ₱17,106. Proceeds will be used for learner-focused projects, including classroom comfort improvements and transparency tooling (e.g., continuously publishing reports and receipts).

The detailed list of payments is available in the official report below.

SFES Holds First SPTA General Assembly and Oathtaking

Date: August 30, 2025 · Venue: SFES Covered Court

San Francisco Elementary School successfully held its First SPTA General Assembly on Friday, August 30, 2025, alongside the distribution of student report cards and the oathtaking of the newly elected SPTA officers.

City Councilor Martin Nicholo Ferrer, Chairman of the Committee on Education, graced the program and served as the officiating officer for the SPTA oathtaking.

During the assembly, SPTA Vice President Grace Echavia announced the approved ₱30 annual SPTA fee, recommended by the school principal, to support projects and activities that directly benefit learners.
